About

I am a clinician-scientist who examines access to and engagement with evidence-based psychological interventions within integrated healthcare systems, with particular attention to cognition, substance use, and the structural conditions that shape who receives care and when. I work at the intersection of intervention science, implementation research, and health systems inquiry — bringing direct clinical experience into the design and interpretation of empirical work.
